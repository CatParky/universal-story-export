Prepare a Story

Paste the edited text, name it, and seal it with your passphrase. Frontmatter fills the form automatically; the file is encrypted before it ever touches the shelf.

  • Reads frontmatter from the Extractor
  • AES-GCM encryption in your browser
  • Passphrase never stored or sent
  • Outputs a ready-to-commit file

The Reading Room

Your private, passphrase-locked shelf. Unlock once and every sealed story is decrypted in the browser — browse by character, scenario or tag, and settle in to read.

  • One passphrase unlocks the whole shelf
  • Filter by tags, character & scenario
  • Everything decrypts locally, on your machine
  • New stories appear automatically once pushed

How it all fits together

Extract a chat → edit & add frontmatter → Seal with your passphrase → commit & push → read it in the Refuge

Every tool runs locally in your browser. Your stories never leave your device.

Sealed stories are encrypted before they are ever committed — the passphrase lives only in your head.
